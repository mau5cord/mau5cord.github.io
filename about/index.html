Audio Catalogues

With the incredible help of Xythium, we've created and arranged spreadsheets for four brands (beathau5, deadmau5, hau5trap, and mau5trap). Here, you can find information for any and all official releases ever published under these labels/aliases including artwork, track lists, purchase links, and availability.

Artwork Catalogues

Again, with the help of Xythium, we've also properly stockpiled roughly 75% of all high-quality release artworks across all brands. We are actively looking to collect the rest but this process takes time and quality control.

Wallpapers

We've made a handful over the past year and have also archived some official files provided by Joel and mau5trap. All files are high resolution.
